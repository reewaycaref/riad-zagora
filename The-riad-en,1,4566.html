The Riad

This Riad of character, set up in 2002, was built according to the mysterious and alive image of Morocco. The traditional materials used in the architecture and the decoration create an authentic world where life unwinds peaceful and serene. Inside the decoration is typically Moroccan with its stone floors of terra-cotta, its walls covered with gleaming Tadelakt, its ceilings of plaster splendidly sculptured and its nicely coloured windows panes. 

Three lounges are placed around a central patio covered with a dome where an impressive chandelier is suspended. In the centre of this hall, a marble fountain whose rustle permeates the atmosphere. Each of these three rooms (air-conditioned) possesses its own decoration and peculiarity. The bar, opened directly on the swimming pool, the Moroccan lounge, decorated with wide sofas and the dining room covered with the red briquettes of Marrakesh will know how to seduce you for the biggest pleasure of your eyes.
