    Coming to Zagora
    By plane, the nearest airport is Ouarzazate. Royal Air Morocco Air Horizons, Govolo, Regional Air Lines, Air Lines President offer discounts direct flights from Paris and direct flights at higher prices from other European capitals. The situation changes from season to season: check the possibilities!

    Alternatively, the nearest international airports are Marrakesh and Agadir (it takes about the same time to come from either of these cities Zagora after the day the road to Marrakech is beautiful, shorter but mountainous; that of Agadir, just as beautiful, is longer but less eventful.

    By car from Marrakech (by the Tichka) or Agadir (by Tazenacht and Agdz)
    In both cases, after about 6 hours away.

    From Marrakech, a magnificent mountain road awaits you on the edge of steep Tichka (2260 m), then descending to Ouarzazate, where you retraverserez a little mountain (1698 m) to engage in the Draa Valley and its superb string of palm groves, kasbah and qsour.

    From Agadir, the road climbs the Souss valley and crosses the mountainous terrain of lowland Tazenacht Agdz.

    From Ouarzazate, after 2 hours 30 drive.
